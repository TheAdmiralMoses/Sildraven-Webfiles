The World of Sil'Draven: A Discord Roleplay Lore Repository

Welcome to the official lore repository for the Sil'Draven Discord server! This site is dedicated to providing a detailed and immersive background for our ongoing collaborative storytelling. Here, you'll find everything you need to understand the world of Sil'Draven, from its sprawling kingdoms and diverse races to the fascinating creatures that dwell within the archipelago.

Whether you're a seasoned roleplayer or new to Sil'Draven, this website is your guide to the world we've created together. Explore the interactive map to visualize the lands and discover the intricate connections between its regions. Each section of this site offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Sil'Draven, designed to inspire your roleplaying adventures.

We encourage you to use this repository as a foundation for your characters and stories, and to help shape the evolving narrative of Sil'Draven.

March Clover Event

As you know we are in March, this means it’s that lucky time of the year, for the rest of the month your characters can complete quests for clover coins. Clover coins are a limited time edition currency that will only be accepted in the clover shop, each quest will reward you different amounts of clover coins, the harder the quest the more you get. And for those who don’t have a character I recommend you make one this is something you only see in March, quests will be updated each week so make sure you don’t miss a quest! Goodluck to everyone. ☘️

  • 100 clover coins:Irp Clover Umbrella
  • 500 clover coins:”Lucky” Role
  • 1,000 clover coins:Creature find luck increase by 5% or Resource Luck Increase by 10% (Can only choose one when purchasing one and does not stack able to buy both.)
